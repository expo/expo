AppMetricsModule gains a module-owned coroutine scope on Dispatchers.IO that prunes metric/log rows older than the retention window and tidies dead inactive session rows without touching the live session. ObservabilityManager gains a sibling loop that calls dispatchUnsentMetrics/dispatchUnsentLogs at the configured cadence so in-session telemetry leaves the device without waiting for the activity to background. Both scopes are cancelled on module destroy so neither outlives the process. SessionManager grows cleanupMetricsAndLogs and cleanupInactiveSessions; the existing on-background cleanup path is unchanged. ObservePreferences and the configure() Record gain the two new optional fields. Adds a Room in-memory test that asserts old rows are pruned, fresh rows remain, and the live session survives.
